How FlowSquad Works

Simple 5-step process from codebase to intelligent artifacts

1

Analyze Your Code

Point FlowSquad to your repository (GitHub, local folder, or upload). Supports all major languages.

2

AI Deep Analysis

FlowSquad parses your code, extracts modules, dependencies, patterns, and stores embeddings in vector database.

3

Refine Requirements

Enter vague ideas. AI transforms them into complete requirements with acceptance criteria, dependencies, and completeness scores.

4

Generate Designs & Tests

Auto-create technical designs, architecture diagrams, and test cases aligned with YOUR codebase patterns.

5

Score Quality

Get quality metrics, risk analysis (low/medium/high), and prioritized recommendations for improvement.
